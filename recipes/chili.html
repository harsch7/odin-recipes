Chili Recipe

Description

Chili is a hearty and flavorful stew typically made with meat, beans, and a blend of chili peppers and spices. Its origins trace back to Mexican cuisine, evolving over time into a beloved dish across the Americas.

PChili is known for its customizable nature, with variations ranging from spicy to mild and incorporating ingredients like ground beef, pork, tomatoes, onions, garlic, cumin, oregano, and a variety of beans.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Beans
  • Vegetables
  • Spices
  • Tomatoes
  • Broth

Steps

  1. Brown the meat
  2. Saute the vegetables
  3. Simmer
  4. Add beans
  5. Serve and enjoy!
