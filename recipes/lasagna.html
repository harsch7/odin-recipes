Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish consisting of several layers of wide, flat pasta sheets. These layers are typically alternated with fillings such as meat sauce (often made with ground beef or sausage), vegetables like spinach or mushrooms, and various cheeses, including ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan.

The dish is often baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the pasta is cooked through. Lasagna is a hearty and satisfying meal that can be enjoyed by people of all ages.

  • Vegetables
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
