Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a beloved dish originating from Italy, consisting of a flattened disc of bread dough typically topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients. It's baked in an oven until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly

Pizza's versatility shines through its countless variations, from classic Margherita with tomato, mozzarella, and basil to more adventurous combinations like pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, and even pineapple.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings: Pepperoni, sausage, basic, etc.

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Assemble
  4. Bake
  5. Slice and enjoy!
